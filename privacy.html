Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At VT Soft, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

VT Soft applications are designed with privacy as a core principle. Our applications:

  • Do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Do not require user registration or account creation
  • Do not access device storage without explicit user permission
  • Do not track user location or browsing history

2. Data Collection in Our Applications

Our applications, including the CSL Calculations app, operate with the following data practices:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from our users
  • Local Processing: All calculations and data processing occur locally on your device
  • No Internet Connection Required: Most features work offline without requiring internet access
  • Anonymous Analytics: If analytics are used, they are completely anonymous and cannot identify individual users

3. Permissions

Our applications request minimal permissions:

  • Internet Access: Only when required for specific features (like holiday calendar updates)
  • Storage Access: Only when users explicitly choose to save or export data

We never request unnecessary permissions that could compromise your privacy.

4. Third-Party Services

Our applications may use the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: For hosting web content in WebView-based applications
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics (where implemented)

These services adhere to their own privacy policies, but we ensure they don't collect personally identifiable information.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it
  • We use secure connections (HTTPS) for any data transmission
  • Regular security reviews of our applications and infrastructure

6. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
