Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Refund Policy

At VT Soft, we strive to provide high-quality software solutions and services. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be provided.

Important Note

Most of our applications are available free of charge. This refund policy primarily applies to any paid custom development services we may provide.

1. Free Applications

Our publicly available applications, including the CSL Calculations app, are provided free of charge. Since these applications are free:

  • No purchase is required to download or use these applications
  • No refunds are applicable as no financial transaction occurs
  • Users can uninstall the application at any time without financial implications

2. Custom Development Services

For paid custom software development services, the following refund policy applies:

  • Project Cancellation: If a project is cancelled before development work begins, a full refund will be provided
  • Partial Completion: If a project is cancelled after partial completion, refunds will be calculated based on work completed and resources utilized
  • Service Unavailability: If we are unable to deliver the agreed-upon services, a full refund will be provided

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following circumstances:

  • Services have been fully rendered as per agreement
  • Change of mind after project completion
  • Incompatibility with user's device or system (unless specified in requirements)
  • Minor bugs or issues that don't significantly impact core functionality
  • Failure to provide necessary information or cooperation during development

4. Refund Process

To request a refund for eligible paid services:

  1. Contact us at vtsoft@yahoo.com with your refund request
  2. Include details of the service and reason for refund request
  3. We will review your request within 7 business days
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed within 14 business days

5. Technical Support

For technical issues with our free applications:

  • We provide free technical support for all our applications
  • Contact us for assistance with any technical problems
  • We will make reasonable efforts to resolve any legitimate issues

6. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this website.
